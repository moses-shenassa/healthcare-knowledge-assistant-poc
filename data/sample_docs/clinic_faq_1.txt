Q: When should I see a doctor about a fever?
A: Adults should contact a clinician if a fever is very high, lasts more than
a few days, or is accompanied by concerning symptoms such as trouble breathing,
chest pain, confusion, or a stiff neck.

Q: What is preventive care?
A: Preventive care includes checkups, screenings, and vaccinations that help
find health problems early or reduce the chance of getting sick.
